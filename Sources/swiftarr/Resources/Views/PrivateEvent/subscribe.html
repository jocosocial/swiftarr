  • Subscribing to a Calendar
  • The button below will take you to your Calendar app and open a panel that lets you subscribe to your Day Planner calendar.
  • This will create a new calendar in your device's Calendar app, named "Joco Cruise #(currentYear): #(trunk.username)". All the events on your Twitarr Day Planner will then show up in your Calendar app.
  • The calendar app will then occasionally poll the Twitarr server for changes and updates.
  • After the cruise, you can safely delete the cruise calendar from your Calendar app.
